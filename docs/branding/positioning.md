# ANNY — Brand Positioning — Source of Truth

**ANNY = "AI Trading Companion — Helping you manage risk and execute strategies. Not financial
advice."** Not "AI Trading Bot".

Brand = **Transparent AI Trading Companion** (Trust > AI). The promise is *verifiable
transparency + risk discipline*, not profit.

## Vocabulary: risk-first, not profit-first

Lead with **Risk · Transparency · Consistency · Discipline** — not Profit / Signal / Win-rate.

| Avoid | Prefer |
|---|---|
| Win Rate 92% | Average Risk 1.2% · Avg Hold 42 min · Max Drawdown 3.1% |
| "AI says BUY" | "AI Assessment · Confidence 82% · Suggested Action: BUY" |
| BUY/BUY/BUY feed | Mission · Reason · Risk · Result |
| "AI made $X" | "Mission recorded · risk 1.2% · result logged & verifiable" |

## AI wording

Never "AI says BUY". Always frame as an **assessment with confidence + a suggested action the
user confirms**:

> AI Assessment · Confidence 82% · Suggested Action: BUY (you confirm)

## Flight Recorder shows

Mission · Reason · Risk · Result — **with** Why / Confidence / Risk / Result, but **without**
Model / Weights / Prompt / Strategy Parameters.

## Disclaimers (every page, footer)

> ANNY provides analytical tools and execution automation. It does not guarantee profits and
> should not be considered financial or investment advice. Trading digital assets involves
> substantial risk.

Plus, where relevant: *Not financial advice · Not investment solicitation · Past performance
does not guarantee future results · All missions include both profit and loss records.*
